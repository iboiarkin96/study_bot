Architecture & quality assessments

Stand-alone maturity assessments for this repository: each report maps the Study App to a neutral catalogue of reference practices (Table 1), scores evidence and gaps (Table 2), and records mitigations and checklists. They complement ADRs (which capture decisions) with a dated snapshot of how we compare to common industry expectations.

Format and location

Files live under docs/audit/ as static HTML, named YYYY-MM-DD-topic-assessment.html. Conventions, scoring bands, and the shared Table 2 legend are defined in ADR 0024.

Industry context

Large product organizations often combine formal design/RFC reviews, launch readiness checklists, security programs, and central developer portals. This repository uses a lighter, versioned rubric in git: reference practices (Table 1), evidence and scores (Table 2), gaps and mitigations — aligned in spirit with maturity conversations at scale, but without replacing automated tests, ADRs, or org-wide approval gates.

Assessments are written for PET-scale scope unless stated otherwise: a low score on a row often means “deferred” or “not cost-effective yet,” not that the team must chase platform-scale features. See ADR 0024 — Industry context and applicability.
